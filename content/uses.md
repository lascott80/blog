A running list of the hardware, software, and gear I use day-to-day. Inspired by the `/uses` movement.

## Hardware

### MacBook Pro M4 Pro

48 GB RAM and 2 TB SSD. My daily driver for development, writing, and everything in between. Fast, quiet, and the battery lasts forever.

### Keychron K8 Max

Mechanical keyboard with hot-swappable switches. Solid build, great feel, and the Bluetooth multi-device pairing means I can switch between the Mac and iPad without reaching for a cable.
