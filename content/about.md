Hey there! I'm Lawrence Scott, and welcome to my blog. I spend my days working with technology and my evenings dreaming about the Magic Kingdom.

I've been a Disney fan for as long as I can remember. Whether it's the classic animated films, the Imagineering behind the parks, or the sheer joy of walking down Main Street U.S.A., Disney has always been a source of inspiration and wonder for me.

On the tech side, I'm a computer nerd through and through. I love building things -- websites, scripts, tools, and tinkering with new technologies. There's something incredibly satisfying about solving a problem with code and seeing it work.

This blog is where those two worlds meet. Here you'll find posts about:

- Disney parks, attractions, and history -- deep dives into the magic behind the mouse
- Technology and programming -- projects, tips, and things I'm learning
- Movies, animation, and storytelling -- what makes great stories tick

Whether you're here for the Disney deep dives or the tech talk, I'm glad you stopped by. Feel free to browse the blog and reach out if you want to chat about Imagineering, JavaScript, or anything in between!
